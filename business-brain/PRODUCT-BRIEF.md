## The reframe that shaped this

Her pack stops at "here are 8 templates, paste them into Claude". That is Layer 2: a pile of disconnected tools. The thing people actually feel is overwhelm, not a lack of templates.

So the product is not "here are more templates". The product is one decision and one build:
1. **The decision:** which parts of your business deserve a brain of their own, and which one to build first.
2. **The build:** how to build that first brain properly, so it sounds like you and you never re-explain yourself again.

Finishable. One brain built well beats eight half-built ones gathering dust. (That is also the honest truth about how most people use AI, and naming it is the hook.)

## The framework (original — 5 brains, not her 8)

Her 8 agents collapse into 5 cleaner functions. Fewer, tighter, in plain language. Every solo business runs on these whether they have named them or not:

| Brain | What it carries | Her nearest equivalent (for our reference only) |
|---|---|---|
| **Content brain** | Says the thing in your voice. Posts, emails, scripts. | Content Creation |
| **Sales brain** | Helps the right person say yes. Pages, launch emails, soft sell. | Sales Page & Copy |
| **Client brain** | Holds the experience. Onboarding, sessions, boundaries, follow-up. | Onboarding + Client Communication (merged) |
| **Strategy brain** | A thinking partner that knows your capacity. Decisions, planning, launches. | Business Strategy |
| **Visibility brain** | Gets you seen without the cringe. Pitches, bios, collabs. | Visibility & Pitch + Research/Learning (merged) |

The product teaches the model, then builds ONE brain end to end as the worked example. The rest are "now you can build these yourself".

Why 5 not 8: her Onboarding and Client Communication are the same function split in two. Her Research/Learning is really feeding the Visibility and Content brains. Collapsing them is both cleaner AND a clear point of difference from her pack. Less to overwhelm, more to finish.
